Backstory
Main Persona
As a result of his parents working abroad, Yu Narukami is set to live in Inaba, with his uncle, for a whole year. Not too long after, gruesome and unexplained murders starts to happen in the town. After awakaning to his persona abilities, he and a couple of his classmates forms the Investigation Team to discover the truth of the murders.
After being falsely accused of assault, Ren Amamiya is transfered to Tokyo for probation. Simultaneously a series of unexplained psychotic breakdown occurs. After awakening to his persona, him and a couple of classmates forms the Phantom Thieves, vigilantes who rebel against corrupt people and to perform a change of heart of the people in Tokyo who became accustomed to being used.
10 years after his parents dying in a result of a accident. Makoto Yuki transfers to Iwatodai to study. At the same time, multiple unexplained incidents involving people who shown no prior symptoms to suddenly gain apathy syndrome. After transfering to a dorm, he discovers the Dark Hour, a "hidden" hour that happens between 12 o'clock and the next. He soon awakens to his persona abilities and joins the Specialized Extracurricular Execution Squad to eliminate the dark hour and discover his answer to life.
